Mid Fade Cut
popular men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back while leaving more length on the top. The fade typically starts around the temples and gradually tapers down to the nape of the neck. It is a good option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and modern. It is also a popular choice for men who want a shorter hairstyle without going for a full buzz cut.
